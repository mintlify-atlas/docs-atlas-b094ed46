## Generating Your First Audio

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Text to Speech">
    From the sidebar, click **Text to Speech** or visit `/text-to-speech` in your dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Text">
    In the main text input panel, type or paste the text you want to convert to speech.
    
    <Info>
      Maximum text length: **5,000 characters** per generation
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a Voice">
    Click the **Voice Style** dropdown to choose from:
    - **Team Voices** - Custom voices created by your organization
    - **Built-in Voices** - Pre-configured system voices
    
    Each voice displays its name and category (e.g., Audiobook, Conversational, Narrative).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Adjust Voice Settings (Optional)">
    Fine-tune how the voice sounds using the sliders:
    
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Creativity (Temperature)">
        Controls variation in voice expression.
        - **Consistent** (0.0): Predictable, stable delivery
        - **Expressive** (2.0): More dynamic and varied
        - Default: 0.8
      </Accordion>
      
      <Accordion title="Voice Variety (Top P)">
        Affects the range of possible voice outputs.
        - **Stable** (0.0): Minimal variation
        - **Dynamic** (1.0): Maximum variety
        - Default: 0.95
      </Accordion>
      
      <Accordion title="Expression Range (Top K)">
        Controls the breadth of expressive choices.
        - **Subtle** (1): Limited expression
        - **Dramatic** (10,000): Full expressive range
        - Default: 1,000
      </Accordion>
      
      <Accordion title="Natural Flow (Repetition Penalty)">
        Prevents repetitive patterns in speech.
        - **Rhythmic** (1.0): Allows repetition
        - **Varied** (2.0): Penalizes repetition
        - Default: 1.2
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Audio">
    Click the **Generate** button to create your audio.
    
    The system will:
    1. Validate your active subscription
    2. Process your text with the selected voice
    3. Apply your voice settings
    4. Generate and store the audio file
    5. Record usage for billing (charged per character)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Preview and Download">
    Once generated, you'll be redirected to the audio detail page where you can:
    - Play the audio with the built-in player
    - Download the audio file
    - View generation settings
    - See character count and metadata
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Error Handling

### Subscription Required

If you don't have an active subscription, you'll see:
```
Subscription required
[Subscribe]
```

Click **Subscribe** to start a checkout session with Polar.

### Voice Not Found

If the selected voice is unavailable (e.g., a team voice was deleted), select a different voice from the dropdown.

### Generation Failed

If generation fails:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Verify your text is under 5,000 characters
3. Try a different voice
4. Contact support if the issue persists

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="For Audiobooks">
    - Use voices in the **Audiobook** or **Narrative** category
    - Keep temperature lower (0.6-0.8) for consistency
    - Use higher repetition penalty (1.3-1.5) for natural flow
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="For Podcasts">
    - Select **Podcast** or **Conversational** voices
    - Increase temperature (0.8-1.0) for more expression
    - Use moderate Top P (0.9-0.95) for natural variation
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="For Customer Service">
    - Choose **Customer Service** category voices
    - Lower temperature (0.5-0.7) for professional tone
    - Keep settings stable for brand consistency
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="For Voice Acting">
    - Use **Characters** category
    - Higher temperature (1.0-1.5) for dramatic delivery
    - Adjust expression range based on character needs
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
